Chronicle :
Close to the Ramana Ashram is the sacred spot of Seshadri Swamigal Ashram, which is dedicated to the popular 20th century Saint Seshadri Swamigal who used to display his mystical powers during that time in order to bless the devotees who attended his Ashram. Most of religious rituals of this ashram are conducted in the garden located nearby which is also a favourable spot for meditation
