Chronicle :
Top Station is tourist destination in the Kannan Devan hills of Munnar. It is part of Theni District in the state of Tamil Nadu Top Station is notable as the historic transshippment location for Kannan Devan tea delivered up here from Munnar and Madupatty by railway and then down by ropeway to Kottagudi. This area is popular for the rare Neelakurinji flowers. The Kurinjimala Sanctuary is nearby. Top Station is the western entrance to the planned Palani Hills National Park.
