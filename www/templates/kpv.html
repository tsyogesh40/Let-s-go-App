Chronicle:
Places where noble souls-Acharyas are interned are called Brindavan. The Brindavan where Acharya Sri Ramanjua was interned in Sri Rangam is considered very important for Vaishnavas, so the Brindavan of the 36th Jeeyar Azhagia Singar Vansadagopa Srinivasa Mahadesikan Swami’s Jeeva Brindavan is in this temple. The rays of Sun fall on the feet of the Lord of the temple from Avani 7 to 12 – August September.
