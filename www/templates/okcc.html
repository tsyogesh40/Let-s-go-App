Chronicle :
One of the best known Catholic churches in Ooty, the Kandal Cross and is known to cure the ill while many believe that their wishes came true after visiting the holy shrine. To fulfill their wishes, candles are lighted on Fridays. The architectural splendor of the Church also deserves mention.
Kandal Cross is a Roman Catholic Shrine, situated at Ooty, in Nilgiris District, Tamil Nadu. It is considered as the Jerusalem of the East. The Nilgiri Catholics consider the shrine as the ‘Calvary of Tamil Nadu’.
The cross erected here is a relic of the true cross, which was brought to India by an Apostolic delegate.
Special Novena Prayers and Holly Masses are offered every Friday.
The annual feast is held on 3rd May.
Kandal Cross is about 3 Km west of Ooty Railway Station.
