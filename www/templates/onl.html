Chronicle :
The District Central Library, Nilgiris commenced to render its service to the public since 26.11.1953 at Near District Collector’s Office, Udhagamandalam..
The Library was subsequently shifted on 24.09.2000 to the existing building situated in 35/A, Government Hospital Road, Sri Aiyappan Koil Back side, Udhagamandalam, The Nilgiris. It is a multistoried Building having carpet area of nearly 570 sq.meter.
