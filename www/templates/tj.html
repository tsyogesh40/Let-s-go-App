Chronicle:
The Alangadu Jain Temple is dedicated to Lord Adinath, the 24th Jain Tirthankar. This ancient temple enshrines an idol of Lord Adinath, along with a golden coloured idol of Lord Neminath, who is assumed to be the 22nd Tirthankar of Jains. It is one of the popular shrines belonging to the Jain community and is located in Alangadu of Tirupur District.
