Chronicle :
Paravasa Ulagam is a Water theme Park situated in the midst of a cool hilly region surrounded by lush green trees and lawns in a calm atmosphere on the AH-43/NH-7 (National Highways) between Salem and Namakkal, located at a distance of about 16 km from Salem City near Mallur. Started humbly in 6 acres of land, it has extended its capacity and also the number of attractions since it was opened in 2004 and it is now spread over 15 acres with more expansions in the pipeline.
