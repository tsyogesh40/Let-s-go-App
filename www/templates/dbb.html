Begumpur Mosque (also called Begumpur Big Mosque) is a mosque located in Thadikombu Road, Begumpur in Dindigul, the capital of the South Indian state of Tamil Nadu. Constructed in the Mughal architectural style, the mosque was built by Haidar Ali. The mosque is named after Ameerunnisa Begum, the younger sister of Hyder, who is buried in the mosque during 1766.
