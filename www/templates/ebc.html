Chronicle:
Brough Church, one of the chief worship centres of the region was founded by Mr. Antony Watson Brough, an Australian missionary. This church of South India was established in the year 1928-29. The church missionary is engaged in various public service activities in interest of the society. Situated in the middle of the city of Erode nearby Panneerselvam Park, the church can be advanced from a Brough road, which is also named after the church.
