Chronicle :
Fun Republic Mall is a shopping mall in Peelamedu, Coimbatore, India. This shopping mall was inaugurated on 19 August 2012 and Managed by E-city Ventures which is part of the Essel Group. It is located on the arterial Avinashi Road on a 3.5 acre site. The mall has 6 levels with a total area of 5.25 lakh sq.ft. Shoppers Stop is its anchor store and occupies over 79,000 sq. ft
