Chronicle :
The Kamarajar Sagar Dam at Kamarajar Lake is a 400-acre (1.6 km2) monsoon-fed water body 6 km (3.7 mi) from Athoor village in Dindigul District, Tamil Nadu. This lake is a beautiful location with the hills of the Western Ghats overlooking it. Fishermen in their coracles, coconut and banana plantations and cardamom estates are the common sights. Swimming is allowed.
