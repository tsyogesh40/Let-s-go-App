Chronicle :
Kovai Kondattam, a theme park is situated 2 kilometers off Perur on Siruvani Main Road at a distance of 8.5kms from the city. This eco-friendly amusement park is an ideal place for the whole family to enjoy with several exciting water rides and slides suited for all age groups. The major attractions are Wave Pool, Family Pool, Kids' Pool, Water Slides, Water Falls, Dashing Car, Family Train, Giant Wheel, Chair-O-Plane, Spiral Tower, Aqua Dance Pirate Ship, Merry-go-round etc. and vast lawn space.
