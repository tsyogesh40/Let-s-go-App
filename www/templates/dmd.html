Chronicle :
The Manjalar Dam is a dam across the Manjalar River in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. Across the river at around 18 kilometres (11 mi) from Batlagundu bus stand.The dam is situated in Theni District, Manjalar Dam has been constructed for irrigation purposes. Manjalar Dam is at the end of Manjalar Road about 5.5 kilometres (3.4 mi) north from SH-36 at Devadanapatti town beginning along Kamatchi Amman temple road. Manjalar dam can be viewed from the Kodai Ghat road, en route to Kodaikanal, about 19 kilometres (12 mi) from Batlagundu.
