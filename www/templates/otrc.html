Chronicle :
The Tribal Research Center (TRC) in Ooty (Udhagamandalam) is located in 25 acres of hilly terrain at M Paladin, 10 kms from the Ooty bus stand. The center was set up for the welfare of the 36 Scheduled Tribes of Tamil Nadu and has many lofty objectives. These include, studying tribal communities, imparting training and orientation on tribal cultures and their development, recording socio-economic, religious, political and psychological changes among them, publishing monographs on their life and culture, developing a tribal museum to preserve their culture and working towards the upliftment of these tribes.
