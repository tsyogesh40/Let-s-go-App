Chronicle :
Virudhunagar is the hometown of Kamarajar (the famous Indian National Congress leader and an erstwhile Chief Minister of the state of Tamilnadu). His dwelling has been switched into a monument and graced with pictures of Kamarajar at every phase of his life. Kamarajar Memorial House is now a permanent gallery that exhibits photographs and personal effects of Kamaraj who rose from poverty to become the Chief Minister of Tamilnadu. A few of his clothes, his watch as well as articles used by him are displayed.
