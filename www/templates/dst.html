Chronicle :
Athoor Sadayandi Temple is situated in a hill cave near Kamarajar Sagar Dam in Tamil Nadu, India. Every year during Aadi Amavasai, (the new moon of the Tamil month of Aadi) there is an interesting festival here which attracts upwards of 100,000 pilgrims in a day.
