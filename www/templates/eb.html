Chronicle:
Bhavani is a second grade Municipality in Erode district, Tamil Nadu, India. It is situated at the north of Erode City and it is about 107 km (66 mi) from Coimbatore city on National Highway 47. Bhavani is also known as the Carpet city, as the leading business of the town is known for its carpet industry.
