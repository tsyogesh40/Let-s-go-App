Chronicle:
Thriumoorthy Dam (also spelt as Thirumurthy) is 20 kms from Udumalpet on the highway from Palani to Coimbatore. The dam has been constructed adjoining the Thirumurthy Hill. This scenic dam is close to the Amalingeswarar Temple; close by are a perennial stream and a waterfall.
