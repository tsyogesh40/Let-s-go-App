Chronicle :
The National Fossil Wood Park, Tiruvakkarai is a geological park located in the Villupuram District in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu and is maintained by the Geological Survey of India. The park was established in 1940 and is located 1 km east of Tiruvakkarai village on the road between Tindivanam and Pondicherry.
