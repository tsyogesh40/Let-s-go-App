Chronicle :
Mekkara Dam one of the dams in Tirunelveli is cited on the eastern side of the Western Ghats. It provides water for the fields of Tirunelveli, Tuticorin and Virudhunagar districts of Tamil Nadu. The dam is about 670 meters long. It is a perfect picnic spot and plays a role in Tirunelveli Tourism with the lush green surroundings of the dam. This is naturally located in the borderline of Tamil Nadu and Kerala.
