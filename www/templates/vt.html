Chronicle :
Korukkai Veeratteswarar Temple is a Hindu temple located at Korukkai in Nagapattinam district of Tamil Nadu, India. The historical name of the place is Tirukurukkai. The presiding deity is Shiva. He is called as Veeratteswarar . His consort is known as Gnanambikai
